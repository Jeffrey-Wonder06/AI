INTRODUCTION.
This is a crossword game, your knowledge will be tested on different subjects.
you are to fill the white cells with the right words(in UPPER case).
Below the board is the direction, length and clue to the words.
Use of hints reduces your score by 2 and it can only be used once.
The hints only reveals the FIRST letter of any empty cell.
Use of show answer reduces your score by 8 and cannot be undone.
Game ends when all answers are shown.
You are to submit when you've filled all the cells in order to view your score.
